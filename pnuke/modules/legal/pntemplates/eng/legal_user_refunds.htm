Refund policy for

If an event is canceled, or location or time is changed, you can request a credit toward another event. If an event is canceled, upon specific request, we will refund your money. Requests for refunds should be mailed to:

Us4Earth Project, Vir2us, Inc. 3001 Bridgeway, Suite K #300, Sausalito, CA 94965 The maximum liability of the Us4Earth Project and Vir2us, Inc. is the cost of event.

Event performers may cancel attendance or may not be able to attend an event.

If event is held and performers have been changed, no refund or credit shall be paid.

EnviroPasses are non-refundable unless all events are canceled during the term of the EnviroPass.

EnviroPasses are valid for a period of 1 year from date of purchase.

All events are subject to change or cancellation at any time with no notice.

Enviropasses and event passes are non-transferrable, except in the unusual case where an event is cancelled or changed by Us4Earth whereby the amount paid for the cancelled or changed event may be applied toward the event fee of another event.

Us4Earth is not responsible for lost or stolen passes or tickets.

When you participate in Us4Earth events, you independently pay for your own food, lodging, travel, insurance, etc.

Most events will have some additional charge per event.

For example, if you purchase a ticket to attend an Us4Earth event in Paris, you will also need an EnviroPass to enter the event. Please check web site prior to events for changes or cancellations.
